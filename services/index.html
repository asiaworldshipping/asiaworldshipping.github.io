Our Services

Technical Management
  • Maintenance of hull, engines and general instruments are in safe condition at any time to ensure ship remains seaworthy.
  • Maintenance of the ship is within the laid down budget.
  • Cargo is safely carried during its intended voyage.
  • Dry dock is carried out the most economical and the shortest period.
  • Provide adequate crew for manning the ship.
Terminal Management
  • Supervising, blending and STS transfer operations.
  • Cargo related operations such as cargo calculation and scheduling of ships for loading and discharging.
  • Repair and maintenance of the FSU.
Chartering
  • Represent ship owners such as negotiating and secure attractive long term time charter for the ship.
Ship Survey
  • Pre Purchase and Hull inspections.
  • Pre Chartering inspections.
  • Vetting.

“We ensure strict approach to safety and security is maintained and promote a high consideration for the protection of marine environment.”
